The annual NECKC/NAKF

BATTLE FOR BOSTON

will be held on Sunday October 26, 2013 at the Boston University Fitness and Recreation Center (915 Commonwealth Avenue, Boston MA).

The registration table will open at 9am and close at 11am for the youth division and at 1pm for college age and older. To preregister for individual and team events online, please use our registration page.

This event features high-level martial arts competition with competitors of various ages for a low entry fee. There will be 3 general age divisions: youth, college, and an older division. The youth division will run from 11am to 1pm, and the college and older divisions will begin at 1pm. The event is excellent for adult beginners, with a focus on providing a solid learning experience over winning prizes.

The tournament also features the unique competition, BOSTON BATTLE, which is 2-vs-2 head-to-head sparring using the entire gymnasium as a ring. This event is very exciting, and we encourage advanced participants to sign up for this team event.

College Division Team Kata

This year, in addition to individual Kata, individual and team Kumite, and the Battle for Boston, we are offering a college division for team kata. College teams of five will compete against each other by performing the 5 Heian kata head to head. Scoring is by simple selection of the better performance, and the best of 5 wins. This event is separate from the main tournament events for which you must register through our registration page.

The tournament is judged by traditional Shotokan rules with one score for one point without regard to target area or attack type so long as the attack is made using good karate form (as defined in the WKF rules), with "light touch" contact to score points in kumite. The kata event is scored via "olympic style" scoring, with side-by-side judging in finals. Kata should be appropriate for the participant's skill level. More information about the events can be found on our events page.

Spectators get in for free, and are encouraged to come watch all the competitions and exhibitions!
