Registration

Overview

The registration tables will open at 11:30am on Saturday, October 26th, 2013, and the the tournament will start at 12:00pm for the youth division and at 2:00pm for college age and older. Boston Battle registration is allowed up until 2:00pm.

The $25 registration fee covers participation in any number of events for an individual. For Youth families, it is $25 for the first child, but $40 total for a family with two or more youth participating. A $10 late fee is imposed for not registering before Sunday October 20th, or for registering at the door.

Registration fees must be paid in full the day of the competition by cash or check. Registration fees are non-refundable.
